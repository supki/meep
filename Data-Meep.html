meep-0.1.1.0: A silly container

Safe HaskellNone

Data.Meep

Description

A Map-like structure that contains up to one key-value pair

A Meep is strict in the key.

Meep k a is isomorphic to Maybe (k, a) with maybeing witnessing the isomorphism
